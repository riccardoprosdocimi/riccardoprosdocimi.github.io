About Me

I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at Northeastern University in Boston, MA. I come, however, from a non-technical background. In fact, I graduated from Minnesota State University Mankato with a Marketing degree and a certification in Business Analytics. The reason for this shift, or enhancement, as I like to call it, stems from the fact that the pandemic has thinned the "separation of concerns" that technology and business used to have. Nowadays, computers and algorithms' tasks substantially overlap organizations' operations and play an increasingly important role in society. That's why I decided to take a deep dive into the computer science field, so I can be as effective and knowledgeable as possible for what's next. I believe proficiency in business and technology is a powerful combination that can lead to impressive results.

I'm originally from Italy and bilingual in English and Italian. Before graduating college, I attended a scientific high school where I mainly studied math, physics, biology, and chemistry. I played competitive basketball for 13 years and am a huge fan of it. I love traveling and have been to 5 continents, including 37 states in the U.S. I'm passionate about technology, sneakers, cars, and football (the American one, as I'm not fond of soccer).
